Case study · 01

MedRemind — Medication Reminder App

A mobile-first React app designed for people managing chronic illness. The core challenge: how do you design a medical tool that feels calm, not clinical?

React Mobile-first Accessibility CSS Modules Empathy-driven
Medication non-adherence affects ~50% of chronic illness patients — the design addresses this with zero-friction check-offs
Warm serif typography and off-white tones make the app feel like a personal journal, not a hospital form
All interactive elements include ARIA labels and aria-pressed states for full screen reader support

Case study · 02

SalaryCheck — Kenya Take-Home Calculator

A free salary calculator for Kenyan professionals. Enter your gross salary and instantly see your real take-home after PAYE, NHIF, NSSF, and Housing Levy — plus your truly spendable income.

React Kenya Tax Logic Data Viz Finance Real-world utility
Solves a real gap — most Kenyan professionals don't know their effective tax rate or real hourly rate
Built with accurate 2024 KRA PAYE bands, NHIF income-based table, and new NSSF Tier I & II rates
Financial runway score gives an instant gut-check on how much of your salary is truly yours

How I work

My design process

01
Discover
Research the problem space. Interview users, map pain points, understand what's really being asked.
02
Define
Synthesise findings into a clear design brief. Who is this for? What does success look like?
03
Design
Wireframes to high-fidelity. Iterate fast, test often. Every decision is intentional.
04
Deliver
Ship something real. Working code, not just mockups. Then measure, learn, and iterate.
